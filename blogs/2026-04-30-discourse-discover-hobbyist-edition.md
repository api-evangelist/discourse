These are small, passionate groups, and their forums are the place they call home. </p><h2 id="unicyclistcom"><strong>Unicyclist.com</strong></h2><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><a href="https://unicyclist.com/?ref=blog.discourse.org"><img alt="Discourse Discover: Hobbyist Edition" class="kg-image" height="1129" src="https://storage.ghost.io/c/7d/70/7d70d59c-7408-4583-b44d-98a43cdfa8fd/content/images/2026/04/Screenshot-2026-04-30-at-12.23.21---pm.png" width="2000" /></a></figure><p>Unicyclist.com traces back to a mid-1990s mailing list, but it landed on Discourse in 2020. The forum covers every corner of the sport through categories like &quot;Unicycles and Equipment,&quot; &quot;Events,&quot; and a busy &quot;Trading Post&quot; where members buy and sell cranks, hubs, and frames. Some threads capture the culture perfectly: &quot;Quote of the day (from non-riders)&quot; has racked up 9,461 replies collecting the bewildered comments unicyclists hear from pedestrians, &quot;Unicycling after 50&quot; is where older riders swap advice on staying active, and &quot;The &apos;official&apos; Muni Tyre Review Page&quot; (500+ replies, 240,000+ views) has become a living gear guide maintained by the community. The whole site runs on Discourse&apos;s default theme with minimal customization, which shows how well the core product works!</p><p><a href="https://unicyclist.com/?ref=blog.discourse.org"><u>Unicyclist.com on Discourse</u></a></p><h2 id="robotime-community"><strong>Robotime Community</strong></h2><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><a href="https://community.robotime.com/?ref=blog.discourse.org"><img alt="Discourse Discover: Hobbyist Edition" class="kg-image" height="1162" src="https://storage.ghost.io/c/7d/70/7d70d59c-7408-4583-b44d-98a43cdfa8fd/content/images/2026/04/Screenshot-2026-04-30-at-12.24.51---pm.png" width="2000" /></a></figure><p>Robotime makes DIY 3D wooden puzzles, miniature houses, book nooks, and mechanical models, and its Discourse forum is where builders gather to share progress and troubleshoot tricky assembly steps. Members tag posts with labels like build-progress, build-finish, and tips-and-tricks, so you can follow a project from unboxing to completion. On any given day, someone is posting photos of a half-finished hat shop, sharing a technique for removing excess glue, or documenting how they weathered a tank model. The &quot;Submit Your Valuable Suggestions&quot; thread (339 replies) lets builders pitch product ideas directly to the Robotime team, and the tone throughout is warm, with members cheering each other through multi-day assembly sessions. </p><p><a href="https://community.robotime.com/?ref=blog.discourse.org"><u>Robotime Community on Discourse</u></a></p><h2 id="infinite-flight-community"><strong>Infinite Flight Community</strong></h2><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><a href="https://community.infiniteflight.com/?ref=blog.discourse.org"><img alt="Discourse Discover: Hobbyist Edition" class="kg-image" height="1120" src="https://storage.ghost.io/c/7d/70/7d70d59c-7408-4583-b44d-98a43cdfa8fd/content/images/2026/04/Screenshot-2026-04-30-at-12.24.36---pm.png" width="2000" /></a></figure><p>Infinite Flight is a mobile flight simulator, and its Discourse forum operates like a virtual airline operations center. Members organize group flyout events with coordinated ATC staffing, vote on aircraft reworks and new liveries through the &quot;Features&quot; category, and share cinematic screenshots in a dedicated gallery. The level of detail is striking: one thread tracking real-world airline route announcements has 3,800+ replies, and &quot;Design Your Own Airline&quot; has racked up over 3,200 replies across three iterations. What makes this community work is how it blurs the line between game and structured aviation hobby, with members earning ATC certifications, following published schedules, and treating their flights with operational seriousness. </p><p><a href="https://community.infiniteflight.com/?ref=blog.discourse.org"><u>Infinite Flight Community on Discourse</u></a></p><hr /><div class="kg-card kg-callout-card kg-callout-card-grey"><div class="kg-callout-emoji">&#x1f50d;</div><div class="kg-callout-text"><a href="https://discover.discourse.com/?ref=blog.discourse.org" rel="noreferrer">Discourse Discover</a> highlights communities that show what&apos;s possible when you combine thoughtful platform design with engaged users. <br /><br />We&apos;re looking for forums that have built something interesting, whether that&apos;s a thriving knowledge base, an active developer community, or a space where meaningful conversations happen at scale. <br /><br />If you&apos;re running a Discourse community and you&apos;re ready for your close-up, reach out to us at marketing@discourse.org. <br /><br />We&apos;d love to hear what you&apos;re building!</div></div>
